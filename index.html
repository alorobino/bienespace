ABOUT THE SPACES

Bienespace builds tiny houses which turn the space constraint into an advantage. The limit on m.sq forces a reflection on what is truly important. It prompts us to cut out the unimportant and the pure distractions. The physical space inspires a mental space that better focuses on what we truly value. Spaces are simple and uncluttered and they encourage us to feel calm and positive. They inspire a feeling of connection both to ourselves and to nature. Our spaces can help us be our best, most authentic selves

And they must deliver. They answer to all our needs and adapt to any situation - whether home working requiring quiet focus or having friends around to cook and relax - all while minimising our impact on the environment.
