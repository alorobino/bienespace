ABOUT

Tiny space living for wellbeing and living well

Bienespace builds tiny houses which turn the space constraint into an advantage. The limit on m.sq forces a reflection on what is truly important. It prompts us to cut out the unimportant, the pure distractions and all that crap we never use. The physical space inspires a mental space that better focuses on what we truly value. Spaces are simple and uncluttered and they encourage us to feel calm and positive. They inspire a feeling of connection both to ourselves and to nature. Our spaces can help us be our best, most authentic selves.

And on a practical level, they must deliver. They answer our needs and adapt to any situation - whether home working requiring quiet focus or having friends around to cook and relax - all while minimising our impact on the environment.
